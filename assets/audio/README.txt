# Audio Assets

Place your audio files in the following locations:

## Sound Effects (SFX)
Location: `assets/audio/sfx/`

Required files (OGG format):
- footstep.ogg
- jump.ogg
- land.ogg
- fire_ar.ogg
- fire_pistol.ogg
- fire_shotgun.ogg
- fire_sniper.ogg
- fire_rocket.ogg
- reload.ogg
- empty_clip.ogg
- melee_swing.ogg
- melee_hit.ogg
- grenade_throw.ogg
- grenade_explode.ogg
- explosion.ogg
- shield_break.ogg
- shield_recharge.ogg
- player_die.ogg
- player_respawn.ogg
- flag_pickup.ogg
- flag_capture.ogg
- flag_return.ogg
- flag_drop.ogg
- ui_click.ogg
- ui_hover.ogg
- match_start.ogg
- match_end.ogg

## Music
Location: `assets/audio/music/`

Suggested:
- menu_music.ogg
- gameplay_music.ogg
- lobby_music.ogg
